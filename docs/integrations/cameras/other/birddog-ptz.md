Cyanview controls BirdDog PTZ cameras over IP, giving you access to pan, tilt, zoom, and paint controls from the RCP.

## Compatibility

Supported IP camera models:
- BirdDog P100
- BirdDog P200
- BirdDog P400
- BirdDog P4K
- BirdDog X1
- BirdDog X4
- BirdDog X5
