# VP4 — Video Processor

The VP4 is a 4-channel video processor that brings real-time color correction and CCU capabilities to your multi-camera setup. Process up to four video feeds simultaneously with broadcast-grade quality.

## Key features

- **4 video channels** — Process four camera feeds at once
- **Real-time color correction** — Adjust color, contrast, and exposure on the fly
- **Multi-format support** — Works with various video input formats
- **CCU control** — Full camera control unit functionality from your RCP

## When to use the VP4

Use the VP4 when you need video-level color correction and processing, typically with camcorders or cameras that require external video processing. The VP4 integrates with your RCP for unified control.
