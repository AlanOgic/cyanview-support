This guide provides an example of solderless tally assembly for RIO / CI0 devices.

## Required parts

* [Wired 12V Red Led](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=3mm+12V+Prewired+red+LED&ref=nb_sb_noss)
* [Toolless Tyumen Wire Plug](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Pluggable+LED+Wire+Connector&ref=nb_sb_noss_1)
* [CY-CBL-6P-PWR](/docs/Accessories/CableCatalog#cy-cbl-6p-pwr)

<img alt="cyanview-CI0-CY-CBL-6P-PWR-LED-Parts-Overview" src="/img/productGfx/cables/BuildCI0TallyLED/CY-CBL-6P-PWR-Plug-LED-Parts-Oveview.png" width="400" />

## Assembly

- Cut the LED wires at the required length, make sure to remove the extra copper.

<img alt="cyanview-LED-Wire-Cutout" src="/img/productGfx/cables/BuildCI0TallyLED/LED-cutout-overview.png" width="500" />

- Cut the CY-CBL-6P-PWR to remove the extra copper.

<img alt="cyanview-CI0-CY-CBL-6P-PWR-Cable-Cutout" src="/img/productGfx/cables/BuildCI0TallyLED/CY-CBL-6P-PWR-cutout-overview.png" width="500" />

- Pay attention to the polarity. The plugs have a "+" symbol for the red wire side identification.

<img alt="cyanview-LED-Assembly-polarity" src="/img/productGfx/cables/BuildCI0TallyLED/CY-CBL-6P-PWR-Plug-LED-Parts-Detail.png" width="500" />

- Push the wires all the way into the plugs.

<img alt="cyanview-LED-Assembly-push-wire" src="/img/productGfx/cables/BuildCI0TallyLED/LED-plug-mount-detail-1.png" width="500" />

- Close the plugs and press them firmly.

<img alt="cyanview-LED-Assembly-press-plug" src="/img/productGfx/cables/BuildCI0TallyLED/Press-Plug.png" width="500" />
