## Menus organization

### Introduction
To access advance settings, you need to press the **MENU** button on the top right.
Then you will find settings organized by categories and sub menu.

One in a sub-menu/leaf, you can:
* short press **MENU** to hide/show menu
* long press **MENU** to come back to the main menu
* press **BACK** to come back to parent menu (right white button)
* press the ⏴ and ⏵ buttons (3rd/4th white button counting left) to navigate between leafs of the same sub-menu
