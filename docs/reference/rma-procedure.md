Cyanview devices are designed and tested with care. If a defect appears, the return process is covered by the [warranty and return policy](#cyanview-warranty-and-return-policy) below.

## Return procedure

1. Follow the troubleshooting guide recommendations first.
2. If the defect persists, [download the RMA Request Form](/img/files/RMA-Request-Form-v0.1.pdf), fill it in completely, and email it to support@cyanview.com.
3. Cyanview support will reply with either:
   - An RMA document with an identifier and detailed return instructions, or
   - Additional questions about the defect and environment. [Remote access](/docs/guides/remote-support) to your device may be requested before the RMA document is issued.
4. Review and sign the RMA document, then email it to support@cyanview.com.
5. Ship the device as described in the RMA document's shipping instructions.
6. If the defect falls outside the [warranty](#cyanview-warranty-and-return-policy) scope, Cyanview will send you a repair or replacement quotation.
7. Cyanview ships you a working device.

## Cyanview warranty and return policy

### Warranty and liability

Cyanview products are guaranteed against manufacturer's defects for one (1) year from the original date of purchase from Cyanview. Cyanview's sole obligation for defects during this period is to repair or replace the defective part or product with a comparable item at Cyanview's sole discretion; if neither option is feasible, Cyanview may issue a credit memo. Except for such repair or replacement, the sale, processing, or other handling of Cyanview products is without warranty, condition, or other liability even though the defect or loss is caused by negligence or other fault. Damage resulting from use, accident, or normal wear and tear is not covered. Cyanview assumes no liability for any accident, injury, death, loss, or other claim related to or resulting from the use of Cyanview products. In no event shall Cyanview be liable for incidental or consequential damages. Returns or replacements may be subject to shipping, handling, replacement, and/or restocking fees.

### Return authorization

Before requesting a return authorization, consult the Cyanview troubleshooting guide to identify the issue. Many problems can be resolved in a few steps without initiating the return process.

All return authorization requests must be submitted on an official Cyanview Return Authorization Request Form. Email the completed form to support@cyanview.com, copying your inside sales representative.

You must obtain a return authorization (RMA) number from Cyanview before returning any product. Products sent without an RMA number will be returned to the sender and a 10% repackaging/restocking fee may be assessed. All returns must be received by Cyanview within 14 days of receiving the RMA number.

If Cyanview deems the product defective, an RMA identifier and detailed return instructions will be emailed to the address provided on the request form.

All returns that do not meet the requirements of this policy are subject to a non-conformance charge. Requirements include proper labeling, routing guide compliance, packaging, and authorization.

If a return authorization is issued and the received product does not exhibit the stated defect, the authorization will be rejected, the product will be returned without credit, and a 10% repackaging/restocking fee may be assessed.

### Out-of-warranty returns

If a returned product is out of warranty — because the defect appeared after the coverage period or the damage is not covered — Cyanview will send a repair quotation and wait for a purchase order before proceeding.

### Returns not covered by this policy

The Cyanview warranty only covers manufacturer's defects for one (1) year from the date of purchase. It does **not** cover damage caused by user error.

Cyanview will **not** accept returns from products that do not have the [latest firmware installed](/docs/reference/product-update).

Cyanview will **not** accept returns for products with water damage unless a Cyanview Technical Support Representative has confirmed a valid warranty claim.
